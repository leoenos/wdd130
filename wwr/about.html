Case Oh's New Ambitious Addition!

CEO of the Goob Lagoon, Caseoh, has expanded his world famous water park, initiating a brand new White Water Rafting Unit. He has taken humble beginnings and created a whole new world of fun with this new unit. Adventure Awaits!

History

Through valiant leadership and non questionable methods, CaseOh was able to make the water park into what it is today in a simple 8 months. Taking a small pool area in 2025, it was the humble park was transformed into his current grand vision.

After the massive success of the Goob Lagoon, the famous water park decided to expand it's horizons and introduce White Water Rafting to this reputable brand with a plethora of fun options of routes and locations. Opening in Summer of 2026.
